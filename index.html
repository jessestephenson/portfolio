Profile.

Hi. I'm Jesse Stephenson, a 25 year old student currently in my last year of university (yay!). I have learnt many things during my studies, including discovering what I want to do. Design and Make websites. Not just any websites though. Websites that are easy to view, convey their information well, and relax the person viewing them.

I have a passion for making beautiful things that help people. Web design allows me do this. I can craft a website that gives the viewer what they are looking for, and conveys the message the client is trying to send. I have found no better way of creatively helping others than through web design.

I really enjoy the creative experience that goes with making a website. From the hand drawn sketch, to the fullsize mock up , through to the final execution in code, all of these stages are interesting and hold their own individual appeal. And the interaction with the customer to help them realise their goal is fantastic.

That is why I make websites.
